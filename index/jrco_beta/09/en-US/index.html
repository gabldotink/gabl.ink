First published March 30, 2023

March 30, 2023

Alas, poor tomatotoe07.

This page only took a month to come out instead of, like, five. There’s so much that I want to get to, and it’s a bit upsetting to me that I’ve only gotten this far, with JoeRunner and Co.’s first anniversary being in two days (April 1). This page especially took a lot of effort⁠—the most yet⁠—due to its length.

Only about 60% of the draft I wrote back on . . . checks May 25, 2021? I really am behind. ahem Only about 60% of the original draft is in this final version. None of these pages so far are super faithful to that draft⁠—I wrote them when I was around 14 years old, and of course my dialogue has changed a lot in two years of my teenage life. It means the same thing, but it’s expanded and, dare I say, funnier. Assuming it’s funny at all.

I have 30 more pages drafted out, and while undoubtedly not all of it will stay, I’m excited to get through them. I’ve decided that I can get a comic done every week, posted on Thursday, while still doing school. So I’ll try it, and we’ll see how it goes.

Once again, I so sincerely appreciate everyone who reads my silly, medium–low quality webcomic. I hope that you’ve enjoyed them regardless.

—⁠ ⁠Gabriel / gabl
