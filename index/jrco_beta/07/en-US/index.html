First published October 27, 2022

October 27, 2022

I’m back! Between week‐long campouts, computer issues, low motivation, reading lots of Wikipedia, tweeting about the Mario movie, and discovering the lost city of Atlantis, I’ve had a lot on my plate. I’m pleased to announce that, probably, the pace of pages coming out should pick up, although unfortunately I’m still not ready to make an update schedule yet.

This page was a bit rushed, and I forgot to preview the rendered image at half resolution, so when you zoom in this page kinda looks bad because of the brush I used. This is the first time the details have been so small yet, and I hadn’t tested Clip Studio Paint’s “Real G‐Pen” with those details yet. The full‐size version looks a bit better. I’m planning to release those bigger images (and .clip files) on Patreon at a later time, but I’m holding off on creating one until I’m able to establish a more consistent schedule.

Who is that mysterious boy at the door? Will I ever flesh out the characters that are sitting around on the grass (that’s supposed to be grass)? Will I actually start posting more often, or will I slip into another slump of not drawing even though I love doing it? Find out next time on… JoeRunner and Co.! Or something!
